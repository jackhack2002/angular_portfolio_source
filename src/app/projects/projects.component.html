The Application for Digital Ambulance Services aims to provide a user-friendly platform for quick and efficient access to emergency medical care. The app allows users to request ambulance services in real-time through a digital interface, connecting them with nearby ambulance providers. The app also features a GPS tracking system that enables accurate location tracking of the ambulance en route to the user's location, providing real-time updates on estimated arrival times.
